# Dictionaries

On first install you download the Russian and English [phpMorphy][1] dictionaries. On this page you can add more or remove ones you do not need.

[![](https://file.modx.pro/files/4/6/3/463bce72ece4e0c7d08127daac334426s.jpg)](https://file.modx.pro/files/4/6/3/463bce72ece4e0c7d08127daac334426.png)

mSearch2 needs at least one morphological dictionary. phpMorphy [has 5 dictionaries][2]:

- Russian
- English
- German
- Ukrainian
- Estonian

Dictionaries are downloaded from SourceForge; speed depends on your server connection. You can choose the nearest mirror.

[![](https://file.modx.pro/files/f/e/2/fe2a6450aaf85a054c506cef07a5ed84s.jpg)](https://file.modx.pro/files/f/e/2/fe2a6450aaf85a054c506cef07a5ed84.png)

[2]: http://sourceforge.net/projects/phpmorphy/files/phpmorphy-dictionaries/0.3.x/
