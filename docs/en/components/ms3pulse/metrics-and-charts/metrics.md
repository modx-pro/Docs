# Metrics and indicators

The **Overview** tab shows five main metrics and a revenue mini-chart.

## Metric cards

| Metric | Calculation | Indicator |
|---------|--------|---------|
| **Revenue** | Sum of order totals for the period (selected statuses). | % change vs previous period of same length. Green — up, red — down. |
| **Orders** | Order count for the period. | % vs previous period. |
| **Average order value** | Revenue ÷ order count. | % vs previous period. |
| **Margin** | Sum of (price − cost) × quantity per order line. Requires **cost** in MiniShop3 products. | % vs previous period; also margin share of revenue (%). |
| **Products** | Sum of line quantities in orders for the period. | — |

**Previous period** — same length, immediately before the selected one. Example: 1–31 Jan (31 days) → previous 1–31 Dec (31 days).

## Revenue mini-chart

Under the Overview cards, a line chart of revenue by day for the selected period (with area fill). Scale and axis labels adapt to the data.

## Units and formatting

- Currency — symbol from lexicon (default ₽).
- Quantity — "pcs".
- Chart axis numbers use thousand separator (locale).
